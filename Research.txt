Research
In addition to academic programs, Plaksha University has established several research centers focused on cutting-edge technologies such as artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and sustainable energy. These centers bring together leading researchers and industry experts to collaborate on projects that address some of the most pressing challenges facing society today.

Plaksha’s vision is firmly rooted in interdisciplinary research to address grand challenges. In line with this, we are establishing four research centers, dedicated to addressing global challenges in clean energy, water security, equitable health, and sustainable agriculture, supported by state-of-the-art research laboratories.

Indorama Ventures Center for Clean Energy
Indorama Ventures Center for Clean Energy at Plaksha is one of our first interdisciplinary centers designed to lead clean energy transition through research. At the intersection of energy and digitalization, we deploy scalable and sustainable energy solutions.

Center for Entrepreneurship
The Info Edge Center for Entrepreneurship fosters a nurturing ecosystem, providing support, resources and guidance at every step of a student’s entrepreneurial journey. From inspiration to ideation, incubation to monetization, and even international expansion, students are mentored by some of India’s most accomplished entrepreneurs and Plaksha faculty.
